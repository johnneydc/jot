Take notes easily without setup.

A fast, reliable, and simple alternative for your notepad note pad. Jot down your thoughts, ideas or anything you can think of. Jot is able to handle pasting image into your notepad and simple text formatting such as bold and italicize. It also comes with an automated saving of you jot so you wouldn't have to worry losing it. Access your previous jots inside the app to search through your past jots. The app is also installable so you could access it directly on your desktop without worrying about having to go the website.
